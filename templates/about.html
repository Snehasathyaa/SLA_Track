Our Mission
Proactive governance, not reactive fire-fighting

Kerala's citizens deserve government services that work — not bureaucratic delays and missed deadlines. SmartSLA was built to change that by applying machine learning to the grievance lifecycle.

Every complaint submitted is immediately scored by our Random Forest classifier, predicting whether an SLA violation will occur. High-risk cases are auto-escalated before they become problems.

The result is a proactive system where officials spend less time triaging and more time resolving — and citizens get real-time transparency into their complaints.
